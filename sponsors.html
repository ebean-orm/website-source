We would like to thank the above sponsors for their support of the Ebean project.

Sponsorship is important for open source projects in that it enables the maintainers to better maintain the project with documentation, examples, bug fixes, enhancements and supporting users. To make a great library takes a lot of time and effort and our sponsors recognise this.

This funding directly contributes to the effort to make Ebean awesome.

Becoming a sponsor

If your company team would like to become a sponsor for Ebean then:

1. Get in touch

Send an email to robin.bygrave@gmail.com letting me know that you are keen to become a sponsor of the Ebean project. Let me know your preferred currency and if that isn't Euros or USD the expected exchange rate.

2. Invoice

For Premium sponsors we will send you an invoice for a year of sponsorship.

3. Payment

You can pay via PayPal / Credit card or pay direct. Sponsors to date have preferred to pay a single annual amount rather than a recurring monthly payment as this means there is only then a single payment for the accounting team to process.

Pay via Paypal
4. Website update

We update the website and github pages with your corporate logo and url. Your company will get increased visibility to the development community with page impressions from both the ebean.io website and github project page.

Past sponsorship

Full time sponsorship

Big thanks to Sean and Accolade from all the Ebean fans!!

Accolade.com hired the main contributor to Ebean (Rob Bygrave) full time for a year to develop many of the features in Ebean we now all enjoy. The ability to work full time on Ebean really helped push the Ebean project to another level that would otherwise have taken much longer to achieve.

The development of Query beans also started with the prompting of Evan at Accolade so an additional hat tip to Evan for that!!

A massive amount of work was done included the development of the following features:

  • History support
  • DB Migration
  • ElasticSearch integration
  • Changelog
  • Read auditing
  • Draftable
  • JSON in the Database
  • Soft delete
